Member’s Information

How to Avail of Your Dental Benefits

Follow these simple steps to confirm your benefits, schedule your visit, and receive dental care through the DNC accredited dentist network.

Benefit Availment Process

Members are encouraged to verify dentist availability, confirm their benefits, and prepare the required identification before their visit.

1
support_agent

Check Dentist Availability and Benefits

Member calls DNC Hotlines or checks the DNC website for current dentists, clinic locations, schedules, and benefit verification.

2
event_available

Secure an Appointment

Member calls the accredited dentist directly to secure an appointment.

3
verified_user

Appointment Verification

Dentist contacts the DNC Hotline for verification and contacts the member to confirm the appointment.

4
badge

Visit the Dentist

Member visits the dentist to avail of their dental benefits. Member should bring their dental card and a valid ID for proper verification of identity.

5
assignment_turned_in

Treatment Reporting

Dentist contacts DNC to report the treatment provided to the member.

info

Important Reminder

Please bring your dental card and a valid ID when visiting the dentist. These are required for proper member identification and verification.
